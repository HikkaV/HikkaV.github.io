OAHEGA PRIVACY POLICY

This privacy policy governs your usage of the software application Oahega (“Application”) for mobile devices that was created by Oahega. The Application is related to face-person tracking and face expressions detection in a near-real time manner. The application is powered by Deep Learning techniques. Key features: Averaging by frames to boost performance; Detection of 6 distinct facial expressions; Tracking of person's face and body; Changing the threshold for object tracking; Changing the threshold for facial expressions detection.  

 

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information 

The Application obtains the information you provide after downloading the Application. 

 

When you use the Application, you generally provide  anonymized face expressions (in form of events) that are used for analytics (only if flag send analytics is chosen).

Automatically Collected Information 

 

In addition, the Application may collect certain information automatically (only if flag send analytics is chosen), including, but not limited to, your the unique device ID of your mobile phone. 

 

Does the Application collect and use images and video from the camera? 

 

Application collect and use only images from the camera to provide emotion classification service along with other on premise services. Raw images are not transmitted in any way to 3-rd party services.

 

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device. 

 

Do the third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

Only aggregated, anonymized data is periodically transmitted to external services (only if flag send analytics is chosen) to help us improve the Application and our service.  We will share your information with third parties only in the ways that are described in this privacy statement.

We may disclose Automatically Collected Information:

  • when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request;

  • with our trusted services providers who work on our behalf, do not have an independent use of the information we disclose to them, and have agreed to adhere to the rules set forth in this privacy statement.

 

What are my opt-out rights?

You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling the Application. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network. You can also request to opt-out via email, at privacy@applicationsite.com.
